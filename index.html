Bio:

A shinobi of Konohagakure's Uzumaki clan. He became the jinchūriki of the Nine-Tails on the day of his birth. Naruto is married to Hinata Hyuga with whom he has had two children – Boruto and Himawari Uzumaki. He has become the Seventh Hokage.
